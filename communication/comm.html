Communications

Sprint

Sprint is cheaper, but you will have terrible reception everywhere.

U.S. Cellular

U.S. Cellular has better signal, but less phone options. For example, they won't have the iPhone 11 Pro, they are still on iPhone X.

Verizon

Verizon has great signal and a good selection of phones.

All plans have unlimited talk. Prices are per person. Don't forget to think about whether or not your job would require you to have a data phone!
